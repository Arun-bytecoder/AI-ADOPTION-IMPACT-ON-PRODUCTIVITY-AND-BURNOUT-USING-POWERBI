📊 AI Adoption Impact on Productivity & Burnout


📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes how AI tool adoption influences employee productivity and burnout risk in a workplace environment. The goal is to understand whether increased AI usage improves efficiency or contributes to employee stress.

Using Power BI, the project explores relationships between AI usage, productivity, workload, focus hours, work-life balance, and burnout risk through interactive dashboards and visual analytics.



🎯 Objectives

Analyze the relationship between AI tool usage and employee productivity

Identify factors contributing to employee burnout

Understand how automation reduces manual workload

Provide data-driven insights for workforce optimization



📂 Dataset

The dataset contains 4500 employee records with various attributes related to work performance and behavior.

4️⃣ Executive Insights

Summarizes the most important insights and recommendations for decision-makers.



Key Findings

Moderate AI usage significantly improves employee productivity

High manual workload increases burnout risk

Deadline pressure is a major contributor to burnout

Automation reduces manual work and improves work-life balance



Recommendations

Encourage AI adoption across teams

Reduce unnecessary meetings to lower burnout

Automate repetitive tasks

Monitor employees facing high deadline pressure



📈 Key Insights from the Analysis

✔ Employees using AI tools moderately (3–8 hours/week) show the highest productivity.

✔ Automation significantly reduces manual workload, improving efficiency.

✔ Deadline pressure and high manual workload are major contributors to burnout risk.

✔ Maintaining healthy work-life balance and focus hours improves productivity while reducing burnout.
